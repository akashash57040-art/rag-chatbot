Company Remote Work Policy

All employees are eligible for remote work after completing a 90-day probationary period.
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week unless their role is designated fully remote by their manager.

Equipment: The company provides a laptop and a one-time $300 home office stipend for desk and chair setup.
Employees must return equipment within 14 days of leaving the company.

Core hours are 10 AM to 4 PM in the employee's local timezone. Meetings should be scheduled within core hours whenever possible.

Time-off Policy

Full-time employees accrue 15 days of paid time off (PTO) per year, increasing to 20 days after 3 years of tenure.
Unused PTO can be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year.
Sick leave is separate from PTO and is uncapped for the first 10 days per year, requiring a doctor's note only if extended beyond 5 consecutive days.

Expense Reimbursement

Employees can submit expense reports through the internal portal within 30 days of the expense being incurred.
Reimbursements above $500 require manager approval before submission.
Travel expenses for client meetings are reimbursed at actual cost; per diem for meals during travel is capped at $75/day.
